Welcome to iRule1

This site is intended for value investors. For those new to value investing, or especially for those new to investing at all, value investing is going to be soooo common sense, that you may become confused as to why its such a big deal! You see, value investing is all about buying something when it is on big time sale and selling it when it is no longer on sale...yeah I know, it sounds ridiculous. "Buy low sell high" - well duh!

One of the tricky aspects of value investing is determining when a company is on sale. First you have to determine what is the companys retail price (aka Intrinsic Value) should be in the 1st place. Then you need to decide what a good SALE price is for that particular company. IRule1 will help you do just that.
